Marunouchi

Marunouchi is a prestigious business district in central Tokyo, known for its modern skyscrapers, luxury shopping, and proximity to the Imperial Palace. It blends Tokyo’s corporate power with elegant dining, high-end retail, and tranquil green spaces, making it a dynamic area for both work and leisure.
